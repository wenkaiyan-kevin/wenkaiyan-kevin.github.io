2014.9-2017.6: postgraduate research

Thesis title: The study of identification pathogenic SNP based on feature selection

Research contents: This paper proposes lots of methods based on feature selection algorithm, such as Random Forest algorithm, PSO-GA-SVM, to identify pathogenic SNP. These methods not only consider the main effect of the single point, but also the interaction effect between multiple sites. In addition, researchers can adjust the methods according to the demand, so our methods are flexible and extendable.

Thesis title: Recognition of 16S rRNA genes in prokaryotic genomes

Research contents: We constructed a 3-layer filtering model based on the three features of GC bases content of the gene sequences, 3-base periodicity and Markov chain to recognize the 16S rRNA genes from prokaryotic genomes. The specificity, sensitivity and Matthews correlation coefficients of the model were 99.58%, 91.60% and 91.49%, respectively.
